Creamy Garlic Pasta

By Chef Rjay ¬∑ July 6, 2025
This creamy garlic pasta recipe is rich, cheesy, and packed with flavor. Perfect for weeknight dinners or special occasions. Easy to prepare in under 30 minutes using pantry staples!
Tags: Garlic Pasta Quick Meals
Category: Italian Dinner

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish
