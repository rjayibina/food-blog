Grilled Steak

By Chef Rjay ¬∑ July 6, 2025
Enjoy a juicy, tender grilled steak seasoned to perfection and seared over high heat. This classic favorite is perfect for backyard cookouts and special dinners alike.
Tags: Steak Grilled Beef
Category: Dinner BBQ

Ingredients

  • 2 ribeye or sirloin steaks
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp dried rosemary or thyme
