Fruit Tart

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 2 tbsp cold water
  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 1/4 cup sugar (for custard)
  • 3 egg yolks (for custard)
  • 2 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Fresh fruits (strawberries, kiwi, blueberries, etc.)
  • Apricot jam (for glaze)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350¬∞F (175¬∞C). Combine flour, butter, sugar, and salt in a bowl. Mix until crumbly.
  2. Add egg yolk and cold water. Mix into a dough, chill for 30 minutes.
  3. Roll out and press into tart pan. Bake for 20 minutes or until golden.
  4. For custard: Heat milk in a saucepan. In a bowl, whisk egg yolks, sugar, and cornstarch.
  5. Slowly pour hot milk into egg mixture while whisking. Return to pan, cook until thick.
  6. Stir in vanilla, let cool. Fill tart crust with custard and top with fresh fruits.
  7. Warm apricot jam and brush over fruit for a shiny finish.
