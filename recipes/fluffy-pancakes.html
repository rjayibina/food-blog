Fluffy Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp white sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tbsp melted butter
üñ® Print Recipe

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, mix flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
  2. Make a well in the center and pour in milk, egg, and melted butter. Mix until smooth.
  3. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium heat.
  4. Pour 1/4 cup batter for each pancake. Cook until bubbles form, then flip and cook until golden.
  5. Serve warm with syrup, fruit, or toppings of your choice.
