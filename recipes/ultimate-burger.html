Ultimate Burger

By Chef Rjay ¬∑ July 6, 2025
Sink your teeth into this juicy, flavorful ultimate burger. Loaded with fresh toppings, melted cheese, and a homemade special sauce, it's a backyard favorite and a comfort food classic!
Tags: Burger Beef Grill
Category: American Dinner

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef (80/20 blend)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 4 burger buns
  • 4 slices cheddar cheese
  • Lettuce, tomato, red onion (sliced)
  • Pickles (optional)
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp ketchup
  • 1 tsp mustard

Instructions

  1. Preheat grill or pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Season beef patties with salt and pepper.
  3. Grill patties for 3‚Äì4 minutes per side or until desired doneness.
  4. Add cheese slices during the last minute to melt.
  5. Toast buns lightly on the grill.
  6. Mix mayo, ketchup, and mustard to make the special sauce.
  7. Assemble burgers with lettuce, tomato, onion, patties, and sauce.
  8. Serve hot with fries or your favorite sides.
