Classic Lasagna

Dive into comfort with this hearty classic lasagna. Layered with rich meat sauce, creamy b√©chamel, and tender pasta sheets, it's a timeless Italian favorite.
Tags: Lasagna Italian Comfort Food

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 jar (24 oz) marinara sauce
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • Salt, pepper, Italian seasoning
üñ® Print Recipe

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375¬∞F (190¬∞C). Boil lasagna noodles until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Cook ground beef and onion until browned. Stir in marinara sauce and seasonings. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  3. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, salt, and pepper.
  4. Spread meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish. Layer noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella, and sauce. Repeat layers.
  5. Top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  6. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Uncover and bake another 20 minutes. Let rest before serving.
