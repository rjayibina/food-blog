Authentic Sushi

By Chef Rjay ¬∑ July 6, 2025
Experience the taste of Japan with this authentic sushi recipe. Made with fresh fish, seasoned rice, and a touch of wasabi, it's a delightful balance of flavor and tradition.
Tags: Sushi Japanese Seafood
Category: Dinner Asian

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sushi rice
  • 2 1/2 cups water
  • 1/4 cup rice vinegar
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • Nori (seaweed sheets)
  • Fresh fish (salmon, tuna, etc.)
  • Cucumber, avocado, or other fillings
  • Soy sauce, wasabi, pickled ginger (for serving)

Instructions

  1. Rinse rice until water runs clear. Cook with water in a rice cooker.
  2. Mix vinegar, sugar, and salt. Fold into cooked rice. Let cool.
  3. Place a sheet of nori on a bamboo mat, shiny side down.
  4. Spread rice evenly on nori, leaving 1 inch at the top edge.
  5. Add fillings across the center. Roll tightly using the mat.
  6. Slice roll with a sharp, wet knife. Serve with soy sauce, wasabi, and ginger.
