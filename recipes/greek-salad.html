Greek Salad

A refreshing and colorful Greek salad with juicy tomatoes, crisp cucumbers, olives, and tangy feta. It‚Äôs the perfect light and healthy dish for any meal.
Tags: Salad Greek Vegetarian
Category: Lunch Healthy

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup Kalamata olives
  • 1 cup feta cheese, cubed or crumbled
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
üñ® Print Recipe

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, and olives.
  2. Add the feta cheese on top.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and red wine vinegar.
  4. Sprinkle with oregano, salt, and pepper.
  5. Toss gently and serve chilled or at room temperature.
