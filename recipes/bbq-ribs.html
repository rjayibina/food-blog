Ingredients

  • 2 racks of pork ribs
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp paprika
  • 1 tbsp garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp onion powder
  • Salt and pepper
  • 2 cups BBQ sauce
