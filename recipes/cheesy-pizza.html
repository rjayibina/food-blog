Cheesy Pizza

By Chef Rjay ¬∑ July 6, 2025
This cheesy pizza recipe is a must-try for every pizza lover! Loaded with melty mozzarella, a crispy crust, and your favorite toppings, it's a guaranteed crowd-pleaser at any meal.
Tags: Pizza Cheese Baked

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup tomato pizza sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Optional toppings: pepperoni, mushrooms, olives, basil
