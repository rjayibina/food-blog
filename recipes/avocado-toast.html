Avocado Toast

By Chef Rjay ¬∑ July 6, 2025
Start your morning right with this fresh and vibrant avocado toast. Simple, nutritious, and endlessly customizable to suit your cravings.
Tags: Avocado Breakfast Healthy
Category: Breakfast Vegan

Ingredients

  • 2 slices whole-grain bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1/2 lemon, juiced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: poached egg, cherry tomatoes, red pepper flakes
üñ® Print Recipe

Instructions

  1. Toast the bread slices until golden and crispy.
  2. Mash the avocado in a bowl with lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  3. Spread mashed avocado over the toast evenly.
  4. Add any optional toppings like a poached egg or chili flakes.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy your wholesome breakfast!
