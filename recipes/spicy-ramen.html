Spicy Ramen

Tags: Ramen Spicy Noodles

Ingredients

  • 2 packs ramen noodles
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp grated ginger
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp chili paste (adjust to taste)
  • 1 boiled egg, sliced
  • Chopped green onions and sesame seeds for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat sesame oil in a pot. Saut√© garlic and ginger until fragrant.
  2. Add broth, soy sauce, and chili paste. Bring to a simmer.
  3. Cook ramen noodles according to package instructions and add to the broth.
  4. Serve hot topped with sliced egg, green onions, and sesame seeds.
  5. Adjust spice level to your preference and enjoy!
