Ingredients

  • 1 cup crushed chocolate cookies
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1 package cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 package instant chocolate pudding mix
  • 2 cups cold milk
  • 1 container whipped topping
  • Chocolate shavings for garnish
üñ® Print Recipe

Instructions

  1. Mix crushed cookies and melted butter. Press into a dish to form the crust.
  2. Beat cream cheese and powdered sugar until smooth. Spread over crust.
  3. Prepare chocolate pudding with milk. Pour over cream cheese layer.
  4. Top with whipped topping and chocolate shavings.
  5. Chill for 2 hours before serving. Enjoy!
