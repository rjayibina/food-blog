Ingredients

  • 8 oz elbow macaroni
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 tsp mustard powder (optional)
  • Bread crumbs for topping (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cook macaroni according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a saucepan, melt butter over medium heat. Stir in flour and cook for 1‚Äì2 minutes.
  3. Slowly whisk in milk. Cook and stir until thickened.
  4. Add cheese, mustard powder (if using), salt, and pepper. Stir until smooth.
  5. Combine cheese sauce with macaroni. Mix well.
  6. Optional: Transfer to a baking dish, top with breadcrumbs, and broil until golden.
